Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Pocket mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, transparent experience for all our users.

The Core Principle: We do not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your data.

1. Zero Data Collection

We believe your personal information belongs to you. Our application does not collect, record, or transmit any personal information, device data, location data, usage statistics, or analytics. Everything happens locally on your device.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our application is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. Because our app does absolutely zero data tracking and collection, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly (or unknowingly) collect personal information from children under 13.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party tracking, analytics, advertising networks, or data-gathering SDKs that could compromise your privacy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Any data generated while using the app (if applicable) is stored strictly locally on your personal device. We do not have servers that store your data, nor do we have access to the data stored on your device. To protect this data, we recommend using your device's built-in security features (such as passcodes or biometric locks).

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time if the functionality of our app changes or if required by law. We will post any updates to this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
